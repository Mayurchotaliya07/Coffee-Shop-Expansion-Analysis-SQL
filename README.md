# ☕ Coffee Shop Expansion Analysis Using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using MySQL to identify the **top three cities for opening a new coffee shop**.

The analysis focuses on customer demand, revenue, city population, rent, and sales trends to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A coffee chain plans to expand its business into new cities.

The goal is to analyze historical sales data and recommend the best cities for expansion based on key business metrics.

## 📊 Business Questions Solved

- Estimate coffee consumers based on city population.
- Calculate total revenue generated in Q4 2023.
- Find units sold for each coffee product.
- Calculate average sales per customer in each city.
- Compare estimated coffee consumers with existing customers.
- Find the top 3 selling products in each city.
- Count unique customers in each city.
- Calculate average sales per customer and average rent per customer.
- Analyze monthly sales growth.
- Recommend the top 3 cities for opening a new coffee shop.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Identified the highest revenue-generating cities.
- Compared customer demand across cities.
- Estimated coffee market potential using population data.
- Ranked products based on sales performance.
- Measured month-over-month sales growth using SQL window functions.
- Recommended the best expansion cities based on multiple business metrics.
